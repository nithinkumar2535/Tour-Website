ABOUT US


Who are We?

We are a group of nomads who were bitten by the travel bug around 10 years ago! Our passion and enthusiasm for travelling, to explore new places, to visit the unexplored corners, to get in touch with the local life, and discover the untouched beauty has inspired us to traverse through every corner of Kerala, the God’s Own Country! Unlike others, we do not promise you to show you the landmarks of Kerala, or offer you the most stunning and amazing experience of this magical land! We promise to blend your holidays with the true essence and authenticity of Kerala! We promise to make you feel home and explore every corner just like a local! We also promise, you will surely fall in love with Kerala, our heart-warming services in Kerala, and will yearn for more and more!

What We Do?

With an experience of more than a decade, today we feel proud when our customers call us one of the best tour operators in Kerala! Our vast experience, dedications towards creating memorable travel diaries and travelogues, and knowledge about the entire vicinity of Kerala makes us one of the most skilled and trustworthy tour organisers in the entire of Kerala. Though we might sound like any other tour organisers, it is our tour packages that make us unique and reliable tour organisers in Kerala! Our expert team of travel guides know each and every corner of Kerala; planning a holiday with us is not only effortless, but is also equally exciting and reliable! In addition to organising tours, we also help our valued customers in tailoring their favourite tours according to their requirement and convenience. To make travel hassle-free and more fun-filled, we also help our customers with hotel booking, creating offbeat itineraries, arranging transportation vehicles, pick-up and drops, and lot more. Our tie-ups with more than 150 hotels across Kerala and leading transport services assure the best of Kerala experience to our customers. At present, we have more than 100 Kerala tours that include family vacations, honeymoon packages, cultural tours, plantation visits, adventure trails, walking tour, and lot more. Booking tours with us is extremely easy and we assure you 100% customer service followed by total customer satisfaction. We have a legacy for arranging the best of Kerala honeymoon tours, family vacations, nature trails and adventure packages.

Our Promise:

Our endeavour Mallu Guide, not only showcase the pristine beauty and magical charm of God’s own Country, but also helps tourists from various corners of the world to plan their Kerala holidays without any worries and complexities. With our expert guides and instructors, you will not only explore and discover the most alluring facets of Kerala, but will also get to enjoy the enchanting beauty and appeal of God’s Own Country just like a local! Besides this, their expert knowledge and vivid experience will take you through some of the hidden places that are unknown to most! Our Contribution to the Society: To make your Kerala experience memorable, we appoint only as well as maximum number of local tour guides and instructors. This in turn helps us to create tours that are steeped with all the essence of local tourism and experience, and also supports the local youths with decent income while keeping them away from any of the evil and unsocial ways of earning. While conducting any of the village and local tours, we prioritise local stays along with foods and other essentials, and help the locals with a uniform and continuous source of income. Our emphasis on local art, cultures, traditions, and practices, encourage the local artists to flourish with their arts, skills and showcase them to the rest of the world.

Our Concern about the Environment:

As a responsible tour organiser, we concentrate more on the well-being of the environment. It is our expert team of tour managers who visits the tourist destinations by themselves, surveys the environmental conditions, come up with solutions and ways to conserve the balance in the eco-system. All our tours comply with the guidelines and instructions of being eco-friendly! We maintain this equilibrium by encouraging the use of recyclable materials, rain water harvesting, using organic energy sources, depending more on the renewable energy sources and other means that are non-polluting and do not harm the nature in any ways! During our nature, wildlife or any other tours, we walk the extra miles to ensure that the animals face no cruelty or rudeness from our guests. Neither we encourage, nor will tolerance any cruelty during the tours!
